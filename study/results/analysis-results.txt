SUMMARY OF FINDINGS
======================================================================

Dataset: 88,052 PRs across 119 repos
SZZ coverage: 103 repos with blame data, 9,754 bug-introducing PRs
JIT coverage: 119 repos with JIT features

Methodology:
  - Controlled regressions include repo fixed effects + size controls
  - Within-author uses LPM with full demeaning (Frisch-Waugh-Lovell)
    and author-clustered standard errors
  - 8 analyses, no multiple-comparison correction applied
  - Coefficients are percentage-point changes in probability

  1. Specs -> SZZ bugs:

    Identified from 2030 authors with treatment variation

    Identified from 4320 authors with treatment variation
  4. AI-tagged -> SZZ bugs:
    coef=+0.0079 pp, p=0.4972 — INCREASES (not significant)
    Identified from 642 authors with treatment variation
  6. Specs -> rework:
    coef=+0.0116 pp, p=0.0012 — INCREASES (SIGNIFICANT)
    Identified from 2524 authors with treatment variation
  7. Spec quality -> rework:
